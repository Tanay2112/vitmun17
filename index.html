Hey I am VITCMUN

"Becoming a Mascot for the World's Largest MUN is not a cakewalk. And 'With great powers comes great responsibilities'. So let me give you a dime tour of all that is to come this October, at the city of Temples!"

Delegates

You debate, you speak. You speak, you achieve. You achieve, you win. You win, We hit the floor! 3 Days of intense discussions and debates for you. I will take care of the socials!

Diplomacy

I have a friend. His name is Gusto. He is a grasshopper. We are real good friends. The only bad I do is, every time we meet one of his buddies, I eat it away! Yummy as they are, Gusto tastes Diplomacy!

Deliberation

The jungle meets every time the river dries. The king discusses it with all. Not with me! Because, I just can't look straight into his eyes. Deliberation in the eyes and on the tongue!
